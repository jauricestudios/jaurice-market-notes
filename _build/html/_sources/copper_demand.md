# Copper Demand Engine

This project examines how AI infrastructure, electrification, and network expansion may reshape medium-term copper demand.

## Core Question

How much additional copper demand could emerge from grid buildout, data centres, and electrified systems?

## Focus

- AI infrastructure
- power networks
- electrification trends
- supply constraints

## Objective

To connect structural demand growth with commodity market tightness and long-term investment implications.
