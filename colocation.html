Co-location Valuation Model#

This project explores when integrated infrastructure assets create more value than standalone assets.

Core Question#

Under what conditions does co-location improve economics, resilience, or optionality?

Examples#

  • battery + solar

  • data centre + private power

  • generation + storage

Objective#

To compare standalone and integrated asset structures under different market conditions.
